project 2: data aggregation

Database: AdventureWorksDW2019

Source: https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sql/samples/adventureworks-install-configure?view=sql-server-ver16&tabs=ssms

Software: Azure Data Studio

In my previous work with the AdventureWorksDW2019 database, I routinely employed SQL to aggregate valuable insights from the data. Here are some key techniques I utilized:

1. GROUP BY: I used the GROUP BY clause to categorize and group data based on specific columns, allowing me to calculate essential metrics such as total revenue by product category or region.

2. WHERE: To refine my analysis, I leveraged the WHERE clause to filter data based on specific conditions. For example, this allowed me to focus on a particular time period for deeper insights.

3. JOIN Operations: I harnessed the power of JOIN operations to combine multiple tables and derive insights from related data. This enabled me to conduct in-depth analyses, such as evaluating product sales in conjunction with product details.

4. Percentage Calculations: To understand the relative composition of metrics, I computed percentages. For instance, I determined the percentage contribution of each product category to the total revenue.

5. Sorting Results: I used the ORDER BY clause to arrange aggregated data in a meaningful way, facilitating interpretation and decision-making.

6. Subqueries: To address complex scenarios, I employed subqueries to perform intricate calculations or filter data based on insights from previous queries.

These SQL operations enabled me to gain valuable insights, make data-driven decisions, and significantly contribute to the success of data-driven projects. I look forward to applying these skills to drive data-driven excellence within your organization.
